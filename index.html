🌈 SafeCircle Privacy Policy — Anonymous LGBTQ+ Community 🔒 ANONYMOUS BY DESIGN Last updated: March 2026 1 Who We Are SafeCircle is an anonymous community app designed for the LGBTQ+ community. Our mission is to provide a safe, anonymous space for connection, support, and community. SafeCircle is intended for users 18 years of age or older. 🛡️ SafeCircle is built on one core principle: your real identity is never known to us. 2 What We Collect We collect only what is strictly necessary to operate the app: Anonymous user ID (generated by Firebase Authentication) Anonymous display name (auto-generated, not linked to real identity) Pronouns (optional, chosen by you) Group chat messages (automatically deleted after 24 hours) Safe places added to the map (location of place, not your location) General location (only when you add a safe place, never stored continuously) App usage data (anonymous analytics via Firebase) Purchase history (processed by Apple, we do not store payment information) Daily streak and gem balance (stored anonymously) 3 What We Never Collect Real name Email address Phone number Precise or continuous location Photos or camera access Contacts or address book Social media accounts Device identifiers linked to real identity 📓 Journal entries are stored ONLY on your device and are never uploaded to our servers. 4 How We Use Your Data We use your data exclusively to: Provide and improve the SafeCircle service Enable anonymous group chat and Safe Connect features Display safe places on the Safe Map Process in-app purchases through Apple Maintain your streak and gem balance Send optional push notifications (which you can disable) We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. 5 Third Party Services SafeCircle uses the following third-party services: Firebase (Google) — authentication, database, and analytics Apple StoreKit — in-app purchases and subscriptions Apple MapKit — interactive safe places map Each service has its own privacy policy. Firebase: firebase.google.com/support/privacy 6 Messages & Data Deletion Group chat messages are automatically deleted after 24 hours Safe Connect private chats are deleted after 48 hours Journal entries are stored on your device only and deleted when you uninstall the app You can delete your account and all associated data from Profile → Settings → Delete Account Upon account deletion, all your messages, safe places, and data are permanently removed 7 Minors SafeCircle is intended for users 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 18. If we become aware that a user is under 18, we will immediately terminate their account and delete all associated data. If you believe a user under 18 is using SafeCircle, please contact us immediately. 8 In-App Purchases SafeCircle offers optional purchases: SafeCircle Plus — $4.99/month subscription with 7-day free trial Rainbow Gems — one-time purchases from $0.99 to $9.99 Pride Themes — $4.99 per theme, one-time purchase All payments are processed by Apple. We do not store credit card or payment information. Subscriptions auto-renew unless canceled 24 hours before renewal. Manage subscriptions in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. 9 Your Rights You have the right to: Access the data we hold about you Delete your account and all associated data at any time Opt out of analytics (via iOS Settings → Privacy) Disable push notifications at any time Request information about how your data is used 10 Security We take security seriously: All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS Firebase security rules restrict access to your data Anonymous IDs cannot be linked to real identities We regularly review and update security practices 11 Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use of SafeCircle after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. Questions? 💜 We're here to help.
